Body-building Programs

The Body-building program is one that is quite popular among those who are looking to gain a high amount of strength and muscle mass. The set-up of this program is to perform three main exercises that target the main muscle groups in the body (both lower and upper body in the same workout), performing five sets of five repetitions. At the end of each workout you can add in a few sets of isolated exercises if you like, but it's not required by the program.

Yoga for Everyone

When you practice yoga, you use your body to support yourself in different postures. Yoga can truly help you develop strength by using your core to support yourself and by engaging your muscles actively to keep yourself in a pose. Yoga will definitely limber you up and helps you tap into your flexibility.

Dance Fitness

Dancing is a whole-body workout that's actually fun. It's good for your heart, it makes you stronger, and it can help with balance and coordination. A 30-minute dance class burns between 130 and 250 calories, about the same as jogging. Sign up for a class
